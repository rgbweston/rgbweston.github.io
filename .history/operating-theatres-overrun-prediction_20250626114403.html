June 18, 2025 — Operating Theatres and Overrun Prediction

In this article, we delve into the operational challenges of NHS surgical theatres, examining factors that lead to overrun events and how predictive models—leveraging SHAP values for explainability—can help forecast and mitigate delays.

The NHS faces significant scheduling bottlenecks, with theatre overruns impacting both patient outcomes and hospital efficiency. By analysing historical case durations and resource allocation patterns, we can apply machine learning models to predict potential overruns ahead of time. SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) values offer transparency, highlighting which features—such as patient ASA score, procedure complexity, and surgeon experience—most influence the prediction.
